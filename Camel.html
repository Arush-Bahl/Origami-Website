Facts about camels

1. Camels can reach 7 feet in height (at the hump) and weigh up to 1500 pounds.

2. They are specially adapted to the life in desert. Their eyes have three eyelids and two rows of eyelashes that prevent sand to enter their eyes.

3. Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.
