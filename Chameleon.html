Facts about chameleons

1. Their feet work like salad tongs.

2. Almost half of all living species of chameleon live in Madagascar.

3. Chameleons vary wildly in terms of size.

4. They mainly change colour to communicate, regulate body temperature or to hide from predators.

5. Skin crystals enable they to change colour at will.

6. Unlike many lizards, chameleons can't regrow their tails.
