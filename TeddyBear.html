Facts about bears

1. Bears eat mostly meat and fish, but some bears also eat plants and insects.

2. Canada is home to nearly 60 % of the world's polar bears.

3. The Asiatic black bear has the largest ears than other species of bears.

4. Black bears can run at the speed up to 35mph.

5. Both female and male pigeons share responsibility of caring for and raising young.

6. Most bears have 42 teeth.

7. Grizzly bears can remember the faces of other bears.

8. The largest recorded polar bear weighed 1,002 kilograms.

9. Bears are carnivoran mammals of the family Ursidae.
